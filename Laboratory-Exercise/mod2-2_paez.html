Your Lie in April

Shigatsu wa Kimi no Uso, or Your Lie in April, is the story of Kousei Arima, a young piano prodigy who, after the death of his mother, had a mental breakdown and ultimately lost the ability to hear the music he played. He has not played ever since until he was forced back into the spotlight by an eccentric girl named Kaori Miyazono who has a secret of her own.
